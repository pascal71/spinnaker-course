Day 2

When you arrive you can already start to continue work on the first lab that we started yesterday.

Some hints & tips

  • Make sure the docker image you are pushing with Jenkins is, is the same you have mentioned in the deployment manifest. (e.g. manifests/deploy-microservice.yaml)
  • Make sure you use the right accounts in Spinnaker
    • Your github account is specific for you and your team.
    • The docker-registry account is global for all teams
  • Test the deployment by pointing your webbrowser to:
    • ingress-stage.di-labs.net:/<your-teamname> on staging. (e.g. http://ingress-stage.di-labs.net/trainer)
    • ingress.di-labs.net:/<your-teamname> on prod. (e.g. http://ingress-stage.di-labs.net/trainer)
